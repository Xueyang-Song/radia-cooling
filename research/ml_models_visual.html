Chapter 0第〇章

What Is Machine Learning?

什么是机器学习？

Teaching computers to recognize patterns in data so they can make predictions — without being explicitly programmed for every case.

教会计算机识别数据中的模式并进行预测——无需为每种情况显式编程。

The Core Idea

核心思想

Input = features you feed to the model (particle size, layer thickness…)
Output = prediction (solar reflectance, cooling power…)
Training = showing thousands of examples so the model learns the pattern
Inference = using the trained model on new, unseen inputs

输入 = 模型的特征量（粒径、层厚度…）
输出 = 预测结果（太阳反射率、制冷功率…）
训练 = 展示数千个样本，让模型学习规律
推理 = 对新数据应用已训练的模型

Click to retrain 点击重新训练
Imagine tasting 1,000 coffees, recording beans, roast time, water temp, and grind size. Eventually you'd feel which combos make great coffee — even recipes you've never tried. ML does that with math.
想象你品尝了1000杯咖啡，记录豆子、烘焙时间、水温和研磨大小。最终你会直觉地知道什么组合能做出好咖啡——即使是你没试过的配方。机器学习用数学做同样的事。

Three Learning Paradigms

三种学习范式

Supervised

监督学习

Learns from labeled examples (input → known output). Used for predicting cooling performance.

从带标签的样本学习（输入→已知输出）。用于预测制冷性能。

Unsupervised

无监督学习

Finds hidden patterns without labels. Not a main focus of this paper.

在无标签情况下发现隐藏模式。非本文重点。

Generative

生成式学习

Creates new data samples. Used for inverse design — generating material structures from target performance.

生成新的数据样本。用于逆向设计——从目标性能生成材料结构。

Traditional ML传统机器学习

K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)

K近邻算法 (KNN)

The simplest ML algorithm. Find the K most similar examples you already know and average their answers.

最简单的机器学习算法。找到你已知的K个最相似样本，取其答案的平均值。

Traditional ML传统ML

How It Works

工作原理

When a new data point comes in, KNN measures the "distance" to every known example, picks the K closest, and averages their outputs. No training phase — it just memorizes the data.

当新数据点输入时，KNN测量到每个已知样本的"距离"，选取最近的K个，然后对其输出取平均。无训练阶段——只需记忆数据。

Click to classify a new point 点击新增数据点
You move to a new neighborhood and want to guess your house price. Look at the 5 most similar houses nearby and average their prices. That's KNN.
你搬到新社区想估算房价，查看附近5套最相似的房子并取平均价格——这就是KNN。

✦ Strengths

✦ 优势

  • Dead simple to understand & implement
  • 极易理解和实现
  • No training phase — just memorize data
  • 无需训练——只需记忆数据
  • Works well when relationships are smooth
  • 当关系平滑时效果良好

✦ Weaknesses

✦ 劣势

  • Slow with large datasets
  • 大数据集下速度慢
  • Struggles with many input variables
  • 难以处理高维输入
  • Doesn't learn abstract patterns
  • 无法学习抽象模式
Su et al. (Solar Energy Materials & Solar Cells, 2023) applied KNN to predict optimal structure parameters and absorption/emission performance of metasurface daytime radiative coolers. Ding et al. (ACS Materials Letters, 2024) used KNN for the design of biomimetic radiative cooling metamaterials, demonstrating that KNN works well for parametrically clear structures where "similar geometry → similar spectrum" holds.
Su等人（Solar Energy Materials & Solar Cells, 2023）应用KNN预测超表面日间辐射制冷器的最优结构参数和吸收/发射性能。Ding等人（ACS Materials Letters, 2024）将KNN用于仿生辐射制冷超材料的设计，证明KNN在参数清晰的结构中效果良好——"相似几何→相似光谱"。
Traditional ML传统机器学习

Random Forest

随机森林

A committee of many decision trees that each vote on the answer. Wisdom of the crowd.

由众多决策树组成的委员会，每棵树对答案投票——集体智慧。

Traditional ML传统ML

How It Works

工作原理

A single decision tree is a flowchart of yes/no questions. A Random Forest builds hundreds of trees, each on a random subset, then averages their votes. The averaging smooths out individual quirks.

单棵决策树是一个是/否问题的流程图。随机森林构建数百棵树，每棵树基于随机子集训练，然后取平均。集成平均可消除个体偏差。

Watch the forest vote 观察森林投票
Ask 100 friends to each guess tomorrow's temperature, but each friend only sees 70% of the weather data. Their average is usually better than any single guess.
让100位朋友各自预测明天气温，但每人只看到70%的天气数据。他们的平均值通常优于任何单一猜测。

✦ Strengths

✦ 优势

  • Handles non-linear relationships
  • 能处理非线性关系
  • Tells you which features matter most
  • 可揭示最重要的特征
  • Resistant to overfitting
  • 抗过拟合能力强

✦ Weaknesses

✦ 劣势

  • Can be slow with huge datasets
  • 大数据集下可能较慢
  • Less effective for very complex patterns
  • 对极复杂模式效果有限
  • Bad at extrapolation
  • 外推能力差
Su et al. used RF for metasurface radiative cooler prediction and design enhancement. Shi et al. (Atmosphere, 2025) applied RF to predict scattering efficiency in the 0.3–2.5 μm solar band for passive daytime radiative cooling particles, demonstrating RF's ability to handle complex non-linear relationships and provide feature importance rankings — showing which particle parameters most influence cooling.
Su等人将RF用于超表面辐射制冷器的预测和设计增强。Shi等人（Atmosphere, 2025）应用RF预测被动日间辐射制冷颗粒在0.3–2.5 μm太阳波段的散射效率，展示了RF处理复杂非线性关系并提供特征重要性排序的能力——显示哪些颗粒参数对制冷影响最大。
Traditional ML传统机器学习

XGBoost

Builds trees sequentially — each new tree corrects the mistakes of all previous trees. The king of structured data.

顺序构建决策树——每棵新树专门修正前序树的错误。结构化数据之王。

Traditional ML传统ML

How It Works

工作原理

Unlike Random Forest (parallel trees), XGBoost builds trees one at a time. Each new tree specifically targets the errors left by all prior trees. The final prediction is the sum of all trees' contributions.

与随机森林（并行树）不同，XGBoost逐棵构建树。每棵新树专门针对前序树留下的残差。最终预测是所有树贡献之和。

Watch error correction in action 观察残差修正过程
Take an exam, review only the questions you got wrong, study just those topics, retake. XGBoost does this hundreds of times.
参加考试，只复习做错的题目，专攻那些知识点，再考。XGBoost重复这个过程数百次。

✦ Strengths

✦ 优势

  • Often best-performing on tabular data
  • 通常是表格数据上性能最优的算法
  • Very fast (parallelized)
  • 非常快（并行化）
  • Feature importance / SHAP explainability
  • 支持特征重要性/SHAP可解释性

✦ Weaknesses

✦ 劣势

  • More hyperparameters to tune
  • 需调节更多超参数
  • Can overfit on very small datasets
  • 小数据集易过拟合
  • Not ideal for raw spectral curves
  • 不太适合原始光谱曲线
Yuan et al. (Gels, 2025) used optimized XGBoost to predict thermal properties of radiative cooling aerogels with the highest accuracy among all tested models. Crucially, they combined XGBoost with SHAP analysis to identify which input factors most influence cooling performance — serving as both a predictor AND explainer. This dual role makes XGBoost uniquely valuable for understanding the physics driving RC performance.
Yuan等人（Gels, 2025）使用优化的XGBoost预测辐射制冷气凝胶的热性能，在所有测试模型中精度最高。关键的是，他们将XGBoost与SHAP分析结合，识别哪些输入因素对制冷性能影响最大——既是预测器又是解释器。这种双重角色使XGBoost在理解辐射制冷物理机制方面具有独特价值。
Neural Networks神经网络

Artificial Neural Network (ANN)

人工神经网络 (ANN)

Layers of artificial neurons that transform input through learned mathematical operations. The foundation of deep learning.

多层人工神经元通过学习的数学运算变换输入——深度学习的基础。

Neural Network神经网络

How It Works

工作原理

Data flows from input → hidden layers → output. Each neuron multiplies inputs by learned weights, sums them, and applies a non-linear activation function. During training, backpropagation adjusts all weights to reduce error.

数据从输入→隐藏层→输出。每个神经元将输入乘以学习的权重，求和后应用非线性激活函数。训练时，反向传播调整所有权重以减小误差。

Watch data flow through the network 观察数据在网络中流动
An assembly line where each worker takes the previous worker's product, modifies it slightly, and passes it forward. After thousands of practice runs, the line produces exactly what's needed.
流水线上每位工人拿到上一位的产品，稍作修改后传递下去。经过数千次练习，流水线能精确生产所需产品。

✦ Strengths

✦ 优势

  • Learns complex non-linear relationships
  • 能学习复杂非线性关系
  • Great surrogate for physics simulations
  • 是物理仿真的优秀代理模型
  • Handles multiple inputs & outputs
  • 可处理多输入多输出

✦ Weaknesses

✦ 劣势

  • Needs more data than traditional ML
  • 需要比传统ML更多的数据
  • "Black box" — hard to interpret
  • "黑箱"——难以解释
  • Must tune architecture carefully
  • 需仔细调整网络架构
Fei et al. (Renewable Energy, 2024) used ANN to predict long-term radiative cooling performance of windows across different tropical cities and climate conditions. The foundational work by Peurifoy et al. (Science Advances, 2018) demonstrated that ANN can effectively learn nanophotonic electromagnetic responses, establishing the "50,000 simulations → train ANN → use as fast surrogate" paradigm now standard in RC research. ANN replaces expensive FDTD/TMM simulations, enabling millisecond predictions vs. minutes per evaluation.
Fei等人（Renewable Energy, 2024）使用ANN预测不同热带城市和气候条件下窗户的长期辐射制冷性能。Peurifoy等人（Science Advances, 2018）的奠基性工作证明ANN可有效学习纳米光子电磁响应，建立了"50,000次仿真→训练ANN→用作快速代理"的范式，现已成为辐射制冷研究的标准方法。ANN替代昂贵的FDTD/TMM仿真，实现毫秒级预测（原需数分钟）。
Neural Networks神经网络

Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)

卷积神经网络 (CNN)

Slides a small window across data to detect local patterns — perfect for spectral data where peaks, dips, and plateaus matter.

在数据上滑动小窗口检测局部模式——非常适合光谱数据，因为峰值、谷值和平台区都很重要。

Neural Network神经网络

How It Works

工作原理

A spectrum is a 1D sequence. CNN slides small filters (kernels) across it to detect features — peaks, dips, edges. Multiple layers combine basic features into complex patterns like "high solar reflectance + atmospheric window emission."

光谱是一维序列。CNN在其上滑动小滤波器（卷积核）检测特征——峰值、谷值、边缘。多层网络将基础特征组合成复杂模式，如"高太阳反射率+大气窗口发射"。

Watch the filter slide across the spectrum 观察滤波器在光谱上滑动
When you read text, your eyes slide across groups of letters to recognize words. CNN does the same with spectral data, recognizing "spectral words."
阅读时眼睛扫过字母组来识别单词。CNN对光谱数据做同样的事——识别"光谱词汇"。

✦ Strengths

✦ 优势

  • Excellent at local pattern detection
  • 局部模式检测能力出色
  • Parameter-efficient (shared filters)
  • 参数高效（滤波器共享）
  • Automatically learns important features
  • 自动学习重要特征

✦ Weaknesses

✦ 劣势

  • Needs more training data
  • 需要更多训练数据
  • Less interpretable
  • 可解释性较差
  • Architecture requires expertise
  • 架构设计需要专业知识
Kim et al. (Optics Express, 2024) applied CNN for deep learning-assisted inverse design of nanoparticle-embedded radiative coolers. CNN's convolution filters automatically detect critical spectral features: solar band behavior (0.3–2.5 μm), atmospheric window emission (8–13 μm), and transition bands. This makes CNN the bridge between forward prediction and generative inverse design — essential when spectral shape matters, not just integrated metrics.
Kim等人（Optics Express, 2024）将CNN应用于纳米颗粒嵌入式辐射制冷器的深度学习辅助逆向设计。CNN的卷积滤波器自动检测关键光谱特征：太阳波段行为（0.3–2.5 μm）、大气窗口发射（8–13 μm）和过渡波段。这使CNN成为正向预测和生成式逆向设计之间的桥梁——当光谱形状而非仅积分指标重要时必不可少。
Neural Networks神经网络

Tandem Neural Network

串联神经网络

Chains a forward + inverse network to solve the one-to-many problem in inverse design.

将正向网络与逆向网络串联，解决逆向设计中的"一对多"问题。

Neural Network神经网络

The One-to-Many Problem

"一对多"问题

Forward: one structure → one spectrum (unique). Inverse: one target spectrum → MANY possible structures (ambiguous!). Direct inverse training fails because contradictory examples confuse the network.

正向：一个结构→一个光谱（唯一）。逆向：一个目标光谱→多个可能结构（歧义！）。直接逆向训练会失败，因为矛盾的样本会混淆网络。

The Tandem Solution

串联网络解决方案

Train a forward net first, freeze it, then chain an inverse net before it. The inverse net proposes a structure, and the frozen forward net checks if it produces the target spectrum. The loss is measured at the spectral output — the inverse net just needs to find any structure that works.

首先训练正向网络并冻结参数，然后将逆向网络串联在其前面。逆向网络提出一个结构，冻结的正向网络检查它是否产生目标光谱。损失在光谱输出端测量——逆向网络只需找到任意一个可行结构即可。

Inverse → Forward → Compare 逆向→正向→对比
Instead of asking "what recipe makes this exact cake?" (hard, many recipes), you ask "find me a recipe, and I'll check if the cake tastes right" (easier, just optimize taste).
不问"什么配方能做出这款蛋糕？"（困难，多种配方），而是"给我一个配方，我检查蛋糕口味对不对"（更容易，只需优化口味）。
Keawmuang et al. (Solar Energy Materials & Solar Cells, 2024) used tandem deep neural networks for inverse design of colored daytime radiative coolers. Li et al. (Progress in Organic Coatings, 2025) combined bidirectional neural networks with genetic algorithms for intelligent design of colored passive cooling multilayer films, achieving both target color fidelity AND cooling performance simultaneously — a multi-objective constraint that traditional methods struggle to satisfy.
Keawmuang等人（Solar Energy Materials & Solar Cells, 2024）使用串联深度神经网络进行彩色日间辐射制冷器的逆向设计。Li等人（Progress in Organic Coatings, 2025）将双向神经网络与遗传算法结合，智能设计彩色被动制冷多层薄膜，同时实现目标颜色保真度和制冷性能——这是传统方法难以满足的多目标约束。
Optimization优化算法

Optimization Algorithms

优化算法

Search algorithms that find the best design parameters. Combined with ML surrogates: the ML model predicts fast, the optimizer searches smart.

寻找最优设计参数的搜索算法。与ML代理模型结合：ML快速预测，优化器智能搜索。

Optimizer优化器

Genetic Algorithm (GA)

遗传算法 (GA)

Mimics biological evolution: a population of designs evolves via selection, crossover, and mutation. The fittest survive and reproduce.

模拟生物进化：设计种群通过选择、交叉和变异进化。最适者存活并繁殖。

Watch evolution in action 观察进化过程
Breeding dogs — pick the fastest, breed them together, occasional random mutations. After many generations: championship greyhounds.
培育赛狗——选择最快的，让它们交配，偶尔发生随机变异。经过多代：冠军级灵缇犬。

Key strength: Naturally handles mixed discrete + continuous variables. Choosing from {SiO₂, TiO₂, Al₂O₃} AND optimizing thickness? GA does both.

核心优势：天然处理离散+连续混合变量。从{SiO₂, TiO₂, Al₂O₃}中选材料且优化厚度？GA两者兼顾。

NSGA-II variant: Multi-objective — finds a Pareto front (trade-off curve) for solar reflectance vs. IR emission vs. aesthetics.

NSGA-II变体：多目标优化——找到太阳反射率、红外发射率和美观度之间的帕累托前沿（权衡曲线）。

You et al. combined GA + Transfer Matrix Method for flexible multilayer RC film optimization, using radiative cooling power density as the fitness function. Kim et al. used GA for multilayer daytime RC structure optimization (layer count, materials, thickness). Mira et al. applied memetic algorithm (GA + local refinement) for selective passive daytime radiative coolers. 2025 results: GA optimized DMDMD transparent colored radiative coolers achieving high visible transmittance + near-IR reflection + atmospheric window emission; metasurface + multilayer designs reached 91.87% solar reflectance and 111.7 W/m² net cooling power.
You等人将GA与传输矩阵法结合进行柔性多层辐射制冷薄膜优化，以辐射制冷功率密度为适应度函数。Kim等人用GA优化多层日间辐射制冷结构（层数、材料、厚度）。Mira等人应用模因算法（GA+局部精修）优化选择性被动日间辐射制冷器。2025年成果：GA优化DMDMD透明彩色辐射制冷器，实现高可见光透过率+近红外反射+大气窗口发射；超表面+多层设计达到91.87%太阳反射率和111.7 W/m²净制冷功率。
Optimizer优化器

Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO)

粒子群优化 (PSO)

Simulates a flock of birds. Each "particle" (candidate design) has position and velocity, attracted toward its own best find and the swarm's best find.

模拟鸟群。每个"粒子"（候选设计）有位置和速度，被自身最优位置和群体最优位置吸引。

Watch the swarm converge 观察粒子群收敛
A flock of birds looking for a lake. Each remembers the wettest area it found AND knows the wettest area any bird found. They drift toward good areas while still exploring.
一群鸟在寻找湖泊。每只鸟记住自己找到的最湿润区域，也知道任何鸟找到的最湿润区域。它们向好区域漂移，同时继续探索。

Best for: Continuous parameters — particle size, film thickness, period, fill fraction.

最适合：连续参数——粒径、膜厚、周期、填充率。

Liu et al. (2025-2026) developed a "FDTD → Neural Network → Adaptive PSO" three-stage pipeline for truncated pyramid RC microstructures, achieving R² = 0.99841 for atmospheric window emissivity prediction and near-unity optimal emission. Chae et al. used PSO for inorganic multilayer selective emitter layer thickness optimization. Bu & Bao applied PSO to nanoparticle coating design targeting efficient RC with minimum thickness. This "surrogate + PSO" workflow reduces expensive FDTD calls while maintaining high accuracy.
Liu等人（2025-2026）开发了"FDTD→神经网络→自适应PSO"三级流程用于截锥形辐射制冷微结构，大气窗口发射率预测达到R² = 0.99841，最优发射率接近1。Chae等人用PSO优化无机多层选择性发射体的层厚。Bu和Bao将PSO应用于纳米颗粒涂层设计，在最小厚度下实现高效辐射制冷。这种"代理模型+PSO"流程在保持高精度的同时减少了昂贵的FDTD调用。
Optimizer优化器

Bayesian Optimization (BO)

贝叶斯优化 (BO)

The most sample-efficient optimizer. Designed for when each evaluation is very expensive. Builds a probabilistic model that tracks both expected performance AND uncertainty, then picks the single best next point to try.

最样本高效的优化器。专为每次评估非常昂贵的场景设计。构建概率模型同时追踪预期性能和不确定性，然后选择最值得尝试的下一个点。

Explore vs Exploit 探索与利用
Looking for the best restaurant in a city. Try 5 random ones, then each time ask: "Given what I know, which restaurant would teach me the most?" You find the best in ~20 tries instead of 500.
在城市里找最好的餐厅。先随机试5家，然后每次问："根据我所知，哪家餐厅能让我学到最多？"你在约20次尝试而非500次后找到最好的。
Guo et al. (Optics Letters, 2020) pioneered RCWA + Bayesian Optimization for RC, finding highly selective atmospheric window emitter structures using <1% of the candidate space. Gunay & Shiomi extended BO to colored radiative coolers, achieving "color + cooling performance" multi-objective optimization with minimal evaluations. BO + FDTD was also used for VO₂ phase-change multilayer automatic search. BO excels when each simulation costs hours — finding optimal designs with ~100 evaluations instead of thousands.
Guo等人（Optics Letters, 2020）首创RCWA+贝叶斯优化用于辐射制冷，使用不到1%的候选空间就找到高选择性大气窗口发射结构。Gunay和Shiomi将BO扩展到彩色辐射制冷器，以最少评估实现"颜色+制冷性能"多目标优化。BO+FDTD还用于VO₂相变多层结构的自动搜索。当每次仿真需要数小时时，BO表现出色——用约100次评估而非数千次找到最优设计。
Generative Models生成模型

Variational Autoencoder (VAE)

变分自编码器 (VAE)

Compresses designs into a compact code, then reconstructs. New designs are generated by sampling from the organized latent space.

将设计压缩成紧凑编码，然后重建。通过从有组织的潜在空间采样生成新设计。

Generative生成式

Generative Adversarial Network (GAN)

生成对抗网络 (GAN)

Two networks compete: a Generator creates fake designs, a Discriminator detects fakes. The competition drives ever-better generation.

两个网络竞争：生成器创造假设计，判别器检测假货。竞争驱动不断改进的生成能力。

Generative生成式

How It Works

工作原理

Generator: takes random noise + target spec, produces a design.
Discriminator: judges "real simulation data" vs. "Generator's fake."
They train together in a minimax game until fakes are indistinguishable from real data.

生成器：接收随机噪声+目标规格，产生设计。
判别器：判断"真实仿真数据"还是"生成器的假货"。
它们在极小极大博弈中共同训练，直到假货与真实数据无法区分。

Generator vs Discriminator 生成器 vs 判别器
An art forger (Generator) vs. an art detective (Discriminator). The forger keeps improving until even experts can't tell forgeries from genuine paintings.
一位艺术伪造者（生成器）对阵艺术侦探（判别器）。伪造者不断进步，直到连专家也无法区分赝品和真品。

  • 能处理复杂多模态空间

  • 输出比VAE更清晰

✦ 劣势

  • 训练出了名的不稳定
  • Mode collapse risk
  • 有模式崩溃风险
  • No uncertainty estimates
  • 无法提供不确定性估计

Diffusion Models

扩散模型

Learn to reverse a destruction process: gradually add noise until data becomes static, then train a network to denoise step by step.

学习逆转破坏过程：逐渐添加噪声直到数据变成静态噪音，然后训练网络逐步去噪。

Generative生成式

How It Works

工作原理

Forward: gradually add noise to a real design → pure random static.
Reverse: starting from static, a learned denoiser removes noise step by step → clean design emerges.

正向：逐渐向真实设计添加噪声→纯随机静态噪音。
逆向：从静态噪音开始，学习的去噪器逐步去除噪声→清晰设计浮现。

Noise → Denoise → Design 噪声→去噪→设计

✦ Strengths

✦ 优势

  • State-of-the-art generation quality
  • 最先进的生成质量
  • More stable training than GANs
  • 训练比GAN更稳定
  • Naturally handles diverse solutions
  • 天然处理多样化解决方案

✦ Weaknesses

✦ 劣势

  • Slow generation (many denoising steps)
  • 生成慢（需多步去噪）
  • Requires very large datasets
  • 需要非常大的数据集
  • Computationally expensive
  • 计算成本高

While not yet widely applied in RC specifically, they have already shown remarkable success in nanophotonics and materials science. The paper positions diffusion as a promising direction for generating complex RC structures with superior quality and diversity compared to earlier generative approaches.
扩散模型代表了与GAN/VAE并列的新兴前沿。虽然在辐射制冷领域尚未广泛应用，但它们在纳米光子学和材料科学中已展现出卓越成功。论文将扩散模型定位为生成复杂辐射制冷结构的有前途方向，其质量和多样性优于早期生成方法。

Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL)

深度强化学习 (DRL)

Treats design as a game: an agent takes sequential actions (add a layer, pick a material) and gets rewards for how close it gets to target performance.

将设计视为游戏：智能体依次采取行动（添加一层、选择材料）并根据与目标性能的接近程度获得奖励。

Generative生成式

How It Works

工作原理

State: current partial design (layers built so far).
Action: choose next material + thickness.
Reward: how well the final design meets the target spectrum.
Through trial and error, the agent learns a policy that maximizes cumulative reward.

状态：当前部分设计（已构建的层）。
动作：选择下一层材料+厚度。
奖励：最终设计满足目标光谱的程度。
通过试错，智能体学习一个最大化累积奖励的策略。

Watch the agent build layer by layer 观察智能体逐层构建
Playing a turn-based strategy game where each "turn" you add one layer to a multilayer film. The game score is the cooling performance — the AI learns the best sequence of moves.
玩回合制策略游戏，每"回合"向多层薄膜添加一层。游戏得分是制冷性能——AI学习最佳动作序列。
Yu et al. developed a general deep learning framework for emissivity engineering using Deep Q-Network (DQN). DRL naturally handles the sequential, decision-based nature of multilayer film design — the agent learns which material to add next given the current stack. This approach excels at exploring the vast combinatorial space of layer arrangements that would be intractable for brute-force search.
Yu等人使用深度Q网络（DQN）开发了发射率工程通用深度学习框架。DRL天然处理多层薄膜设计的顺序决策特性——智能体学习给定当前堆叠后应添加什么材料。这种方法擅长探索暴力搜索无法处理的庞大层排列组合空间。
